Grace Hopper Conference

You may ask, "Why are you putting GHC in your portfolio section?" and here is why. I often told my story as initially I wanted to study Graphic Design but I loved technology too much therefore I went with Computer Science. Galactic Takeover

During the first semester of my Junior year at CSULB, I was tasked an assignment in which my team and I would convert a board game to an iOS application. For this assignment I was the lead Graphic and UI/UX Designer. Throughout the creative process I utilized programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Swfit, and XCode to make the project come together. I will admit that my team and I never fully finished the game, however all of the art and layout assets were completed and as a result we recieved an A on the project based off creativity and creation effectiveness.

Fox Run

During the Summer of 2019, I joined the BUILT BY GIRLS program. With this program I was partnered with a mentor who was a Senior Software Engineer at Niantic Inc. I met with this mentor several times and he instructed me to build a game from scratch. After watching some tutorials, doing some readings, and working with my mentor, I was able to create a fully functional game. The game allows for the user to run, jump, crouch, collect coins, die, and ultimately win the game.
